About Us

Palm From Indonesia is focused in producing palm products especially sugar and its derivatives. We are a trusted palm supplier that located in Tangerang, Banten-Indonesia. Our mission is “Dedicating the best of Indonesia’s natural resources to the world.” and our values are Quality, Healthy and Integrity. We really looking forward to be your trusted partner and will gladly help to fulfill your palm products demands. Palm From Indonesia wants to be your trusted and reliable business partner for all your palm products inquiries. We will be happy to be your business partner and supplier who provide the best quality of palm products you can get.
